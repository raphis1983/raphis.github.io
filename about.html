About me...

Hi! My name is Rafal Szczytko, I live in Poland and create illustrations using digital  and traditional art. ​All my works I sign with the nickname "Raphis" or with an inverted "R".
In my artworks you can usually find a reflection of an alternative reality. I use elements of surrealism, abstraction, science fiction, pop art and retro. ​I don't try to limit myself but to experiment with different types of 2D and 3D techniques.

The tools I use: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Blender 3D, GrafX2.
